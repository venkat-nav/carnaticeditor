Carnatic notation builder

Pick a tala, type each beat's swara group, click a note to set its octave, and check your counts as you go. Your work saves automatically in this browser.

80 bpm
Each beat = 4 counts. Type the group per beat, e.g. S,d or pgrd. Lowercase = 1 count, capital = 2 counts, , = +1, ; = +2. A leading , or ; carries the previous beat's note forward. Click a note chip below the box to cycle its octave (middle → upper dot → lower dot).
